ZebraByte - Free Accessibility Widget
  • Forever Free
  • Installation with a single line of code
  • Works for popular site builders such as Squarespace, Wix, and Weebly
  • No registration needed and 100% free of any tracking

This page is intentionally simple. The point of the demo is the widget itself: click the accessibility button in the bottom-left corner and test the live menu, contrast controls, reading helpers, and keyboard interaction on a minimal page.

Accessibility statement

This demo page exists to show the widget live. In production, this section can be replaced by the customer's formal accessibility statement.
